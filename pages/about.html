About LinuxDroid

Empowering Android devices with the full potential of Linux, one installation at a time

Our Mission

LinuxDroid aims to bridge the gap between mobile and desktop computing by bringing the full power of Linux to Android devices. We believe in making Linux accessible to everyone, regardless of their device or technical expertise.

Core Values

  • Open Source: We believe in transparent and community-driven development
  • Accessibility: Making Linux available to all Android users
  • Innovation: Pushing the boundaries of mobile computing
  • Community: Building a supportive ecosystem for users and developers

Project Timeline

Project Inception

2023 Q3

LinuxDroid was conceived as a solution to bring desktop Linux capabilities to Android devices, making powerful computing tools accessible to mobile users.

First Alpha Release

2023 Q4

Initial release featuring basic CLI support for Ubuntu and Kali Linux, establishing the foundation for future development.

GUI Support Launch

2024 Q1

Major milestone: Introduction of desktop environment support with XFCE, LXDE, and MATE, bringing full Linux desktop experience to Android.

Distribution Expansion

2024 Q1

Added support for multiple distributions including Arch, Debian, Void, Alpine, and Fedora, expanding user choice and capabilities.

Performance Optimization

2024 Q2

Significant improvements in resource management and system performance, enhanced GUI responsiveness, and reduced memory footprint.

Community Growth

Present

Growing community of users and developers, continuous improvements in stability, features, and user experience.
